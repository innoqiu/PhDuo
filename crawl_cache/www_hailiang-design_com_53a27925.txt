#### School of Design  
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University

Hi, welcome to my F.I.T. Design Group~

I'm Hailiang WANG, an Assistant Professor (Presidential Young Scholar) in the School of Design at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). My research mainly focuses on applying human factors and ergonomics, smart technology (e.g., VR/AR, sensor, AIoT), and big data techniques to design healthcare systems/devices, improve human-technology interactions, and further enhance people’s safety and health.

​

My professional services include:

*   General Secretary of The Hong Kong Ergonomics Society, HK
    
*   Committee Member, Chinese Association of Ergonomics Societies
    
*   Deputy Director, the Intelligent Design and Innovation, PolyU-Qianhai Disruptive Technology and Innovation Research Centre
    
*   Deputy Director, Research Institute for Design-driven Entrepreneurship (RIDE), PolyU
    
*   Deputy Specialism Leader of MDes Intelligent Systems Design (ISD), PolyU
    

### Research Interests

· Human Factors and Ergonomics 

· Human-Technology Interaction

· Digital Health

· Gerontechnology Design

· Design Intelligence

· Human Psychosocial Behaviors

RESEARCH POSITIONS
------------------

Our F.I.T. Design Group is looking for Ph.D. students, research assistants/associates, and student helpers. Interested candidates could send CVs, transcripts, and publications (if any) to Dr. WANG for consideration 😎

🎓P.h.D Student Requirements (2024 full)
----------------------------------------

1- University ranking

*   QS/THE/ARWU Top 200 universities, OR
    
*   Top 50 subject universities, OR
    
*   First-tier national universities
    

2- Undergraduate GPA ≥ 3.6/4.0 or Top 10% of class

3- Excellent academic performance and research ability:

*   Two JCR Q1/Q2 Journal Papers (as first/second/corresponding author), AND/OR
    
*   Merits/awards at the national level/university level
    

4- English: at least IELTS (6.5)

📝Research Assistant/Research Associate Requirements
----------------------------------------------------

1- Honors degree in Computer Science, Industrial Engineering, Interaction Design, Human-Computer Interaction, Interactive Media, or related disciplines or an equivalent qualification;

2- Technical abilities in developing VR, or Electronic and Information Engineering;

3- Experience in academic research;

4- Good interpersonal and communication skills;

5- The ability to work independently and meet the work schedule.
